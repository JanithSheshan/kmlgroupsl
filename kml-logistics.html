KML Logistics (Pvt) Ltd

KML Logistics (Pvt) Ltd is a leader in transportation and logistics services, ensuring efficient and reliable movement of goods across Sri Lanka. With a robust infrastructure and cutting-edge technology, we provide customized logistics solutions that support businesses in their growth journey.

  • Comprehensive supply chain management.
  • Efficient and reliable transportation networks.
  • Technology-driven tracking and real-time logistics management.

Our logistics services cover every aspect of the supply chain, from warehousing and distribution to last-mile delivery, ensuring products reach their destination on time and in perfect condition.

With a commitment to operational excellence and customer satisfaction, KML Logistics (Pvt) Ltd sets the benchmark for logistics innovation and service quality in Sri Lanka.
