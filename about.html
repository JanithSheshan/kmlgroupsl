About Us – 60 Years of Leadership and Excellence

Since 1966, KML Group has been a cornerstone of Sri Lanka’s corporate landscape, leading the way in logistics, distribution, investments, and media innovation.

  • Legacy of Trust: Over six decades of commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
  • Industry Leadership: Pioneering advancements across logistics, investments, and retail distribution.
  • Innovation & Growth: Continuously evolving to meet the demands of a dynamic marketplace.

Building a Sustainable Future

At KML Group, we believe in the power of collaboration and forward-thinking strategies. Our focus on sustainable growth, innovative solutions, and long-term partnerships drives our success in an ever-changing world.

Join us as we continue to shape industries, inspire innovation, and create new opportunities for businesses and communities across Sri Lanka.
